Syllabus

Borel - Cantelli lemmas $\&$ Kolmogorov's $0-1$ law
Modes of convergence Different modes of convergence $\&$ their relations.
Law of large numbers Weak Law of large numbers, Strong Law of large numbers (proof under finite variance).
Central limit theorem Characteristic functions, properties, inversion formula and Levy continuity theorem, CLT in i.i.d. finite variance case. Slutsky's Theorem.
Introduction to Finite Markov chains Definition. Random mapping representation. Examples. Irreducibility and aperiodicity. Stationary distribution and reversibility. Random walks on graphs.
